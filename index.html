Partner Operations — Design Demos

Three directions for the partner platform

Three deliberately distinct directions — each a different theme, navigation model and layout, not three skins of one. Every direction includes a Dashboard and a Partners screen on the same live data so they stay comparable. All branded as P2P Connect with a white-label ready brand slot that each ISV tenant (e.g. Cygnet.One) reskins.

1

Data-Dense Analytics

Light · command center · for data-driven managers

Left sidebar, light canvas. Eight KPIs with sparklines, a dual-line pipeline area chart, a tier-mix donut, plus a richer Partners table with health sparklines, win-rate bars, ARR and last-activity. Powerful and information-rich.

Dashboard Partners
2

Midnight Aurora

Dark · glassmorphic · gradient glow

Deep space-navy canvas with aurora blooms, a floating glass icon-rail, frosted cards and glowing blue→violet→pink data viz. The dramatic "wow" option — premium fintech/AI mood, a completely different silhouette.

Dashboard Partners
3

Editorial Horizon

Light · top-nav, no sidebar · warm bento

Horizontal top navigation (no sidebar), warm parchment palette in teal + coral, an asymmetric bento grid and oversized Space Grotesk display type. Airy, expressive, magazine-like — a marketing-grade feel.
